Best places to visit in Kerala


Munnar

Major Tourist Attractions in Munnar: Anamudi Peak, Kurinhimala Waterfalls, Echo Point, Observatory Hill, and Power House Waterfalls are the major sightseeing places of Munnar.

Munnar is among the most amazing hill towns of South India, located on the tropical Western Ghats. Some of the prime attractions of the place are lush green tea estates, dense forest regions, rare wildlife, beautiful lakes, and various guest houses. This tranquil place is visited by a huge number of native and global travelers regularly.

Palakkad

Major Tourist Attractions in Palakkad: Kalpathy Temple, Malampuzha Dam, Parambikulam, and Kunchan Smarakam.

Palakkad is a popular tourist destination in Kerala. It nestles in the foot of the Western Ghats and is one of the most productive and densely forested lands in the state. The land’s untouched ambiance leaves a lasting impression on visitors.

Thiruvananthapuram

Major Tourist Attractions: Kovalam Palace, Varkala Beach, Napier Museum, Akkulam Lake, and Kuthiramalika Palace.

Thiruvananthapuram, also known as the ‘God’s Own Country,’ is among the best places in Kerala. The city is famous for its British colonial architecture, art galleries, and rich cultural heritage, making it a destination for history lovers.
